Over the longer term, the rate of increase is a bit more uncertain, although there is no reason to believe it will not remain nearly constant for at least 10 years. ”

Peter Diamandis (Singularity University)

  1. Way to think about exponential technologies and how the affect our world
  2. Want you to see the process from a slightly different perspective
  3. Digitized: Becomes exponentially empowered, starts to grow at an exponential rate
  4. Digitized: Pretty much everything is digital today, bio, finance ...
  5. Deceptive: Exponential growth unnoticed (truegerisch) - kodak 0.001 / 0.002 -- 10
  6. Disruptive: Innovation that creates a new market and disrupts the old - netflix vs. dvd Verleih
  7. Dematerialize: Vanishing of goods / Apps (Navi, Music, Camera), Cloud Computing
  8. Demonetize: Taking the money out of the equation (Craigslist, skype, amazon, Google research)
  9. Democratize: Costs drop and are available to everyone (New marketplaces, explode )
  10. Democratize: the world of electronic trading (platforms)
